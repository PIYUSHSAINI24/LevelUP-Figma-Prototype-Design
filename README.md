LevelUP is a **student-focused wellness and productivity prototype**, designed during the **Design Thinking & Figma Summer Training Internship** at Lovely Professional University. The project addresses a major problem faced by college students: maintaining a healthy lifestyle alongside tight schedules, academic stress, and lack of motivation.

## ğŸš€ Project Overview
Students often skip meals, avoid workouts, drink less water, and face high stress due to academic pressure. Existing fitness apps are either **time-consuming**, **complex**, or **not designed for students**.

LevelUP provides:
- Quick, no-equipment workouts  
- Simple nutrition guidance  
- Hydration reminders  
- Habit and streak tracking  
- Gamified wellness experience  

Designed using **Design Thinking methodology** and prototyped in **Figma**, LevelUP makes fitness practical, engaging, and achievable.
